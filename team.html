Principal Investigator

Bio

Dr. Zhongrui Wang is an Assistant Professor in Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, the University of Hong Kong. He received both B. Eng (First-class Honor) and Ph.D. from Nanyang Technological University in Singapore, advised by Prof. Hongyu Yu (SUSTC page), Prof. Haibin Su (HKUST page), and Prof. Kin Leong Pey (SUTD page). Prior to join HKU, he was a postdoc at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, working with Prof. Joshua Jianhua Yang (USC page) and Prof. Qiangfei Xia (UMass page).
